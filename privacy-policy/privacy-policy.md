# Privacy Policy for Champaign Bus Tracker

**Last updated: 2025-12-18**

Champaign Bus Tracker (“the App”) is developed to help users view real-time bus locations, stops, and routes. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Location Information
The App may request access to your device’s location only for the purpose of displaying your approximate position on the map (via Google Maps). Location data is used temporarily in the app. Location data is **not stored**, **not logged**, **not transmitted to any server**, and **not shared** by the developer.

### 1.2 Ad Data (AdMob)
The App uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, approximate location, ad interaction data, and diagnostic information. This data is processed by Google to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

### 1.3 No Other Data Collected
The App does not collect names, email addresses, accounts, contacts, photos, files, usage analytics, or any other personal data.

---

## 2. How Your Information Is Used
Location is used only on-device. AdMob data is processed by Google. The developer does not store or share any data.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services
- **Google Maps SDK** – <https://policies.google.com/privacy>
- **Google AdMob** – <https://policies.google.com/privacy>

---

## 4. Data Retention
No data is stored by the developer. AdMob may retain data per Google policy.

---

## 5. Security
No personal data is stored by the developer. Third-party services handle their own data security.
